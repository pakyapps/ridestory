Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

The app is not working as expected. What should I send?

Email us a short description of what happened, the steps that led to it, your iPhone model, iOS version and RideStory app version. A screenshot is helpful when it does not contain sensitive information.

How do I manage location or photo access?

Open iPhone Settings, select Privacy & Security, choose the relevant permission and update RideStory’s access. Some app features may not work when a required permission is disabled.

How do I request access to or deletion of my data?

Email 77pask@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and state whether you want access, correction or deletion. We may ask for limited information to verify the request.

How do I delete my RideStory account?

Use the account deletion option inside the app, if available, or email 77pask@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my RideStory account”. Account data will be deleted or anonymised, except where retention is required by law or needed to resolve security and fraud matters.

Can I get support from the United Kingdom?

Yes. RideStory support is available to UK users. UK privacy requests are handled under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018 as described in our Privacy Policy.

Safety first

RideStory is not an emergency service. Do not use the app as a substitute for safe riding practices, official maps, road signs or emergency services. In an emergency, contact the local emergency number immediately.
